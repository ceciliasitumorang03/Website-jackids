Our Location

Jackids Alam Sutera, Samesta Mahata, Petals, South Tangerang, provides fun learning activities for children aged 1–9 years to support their motor, cognitive, language, sensory, and social development.

Learning & Growth

Jackids is a safe and fun all in one center for playground, education, daycare, and talent classes, offering activities like Art & Craft, STEM, English, Taekwondo, Lego, Role Play, and Mandarin.

All in One Concept

Facility

Education

Gross Motoric

Gross motor skills are abilities that is usually acquired during childhood as part of a child's motor learning. By the time them reach the age of two, almost all children are able to stand, walk and run, walk up stairs.

Linguistic

Reading time activities help children improve their listening, communication, and social interaction skills in a fun and engaging environment. Through storytelling sessions, children learn to focus, express curiosity, and build confidence while interacting with teachers and friends. Group learning activities like this also encourage imagination, emotional development, and early language skills in a warm and supportive classroom atmosphere.

Interactive

Interactive group activities encourage children to explore, communicate, and learn together in a fun environment. Through hands-on experiences, children develop social interaction, confidence, teamwork, and curiosity while enjoying exciting learning moments with friends and teachers.

Sensoric

Sensory is the ability of the body to catch or often called senses or sensing. There are five sensing known so far, ranging from sight, hearing, smell, taste and touch.
Then there is also the mention of additional senses outside the five senses namely the vestibular senses or balance. In the past only five senses were examined. Even though there are others, one of which is balance.

Fine Motoric

Sensory and fine motor activities encourage children to strengthen hand coordination, concentration, and problem-solving skills through interactive play. These engaging experiences support early childhood development in a fun, colorful, and stimulating environment.
